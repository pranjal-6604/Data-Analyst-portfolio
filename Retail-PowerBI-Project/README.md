# Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard(Power BI)

## Objective

Analyze retail sales and customer data to identify business performance trends, customer behavior, and profitability insights for data-driven decision making.

## Steps Performed

- Data cleaning and transformation(handled date formats, ensured correct data types)
- KPI creation (Revenue, Profit, Orders, Customers, Average Order Value)
- Data modelling in Power BI(relationships between orders, customers and products)
- Dashboard development with interactive visuals and slicers

## Key Insights

- Consumer segment generates highest revenue and has the highest number of orders among all customer segments
- Identified high-performing regions and product categories based on sales and profit
- Observed sales trends over time to detect growth patterns and fluctuations
- Highlighted top customers contributing significantly to total revenue

## Tools Used

Power BI, DAX(basic calculations), Data Modeling
